Symptoms of Covid-19

Cough

Coughing is another key symptom, but it's not just any cough, said Schaffner. It should be a dry cough that you feel in your chest.

High Fever

Fever is a key symptom, experts say. Don't fixate on a number, but know it's really not a fever until your temperature reaches at least 390C.

Headache

Fever is a key symptom, experts say. Don't fixate on a number, but know it's really not a fever until your temperature reaches at least 390C.

Difficulty In Breathing

Around 1 out of every 6 people who gets COVID- 19 becomes seriously ill and develops difficulty breathing or shortness of breath.

Sore Throat

You feel hot to touch on your chest or back. It is a common sign and also may appear in 2-10 days if you are affected.
